---- MODULE SeamReportComplete ----
EXTENDS Integers, FiniteSets, TLC

CONSTANTS
    Edges,
    MismatchEdges,
    UnresolvedEdges

ASSUME
    /\ MismatchEdges \subseteq Edges
    /\ UnresolvedEdges \subseteq Edges
    /\ MismatchEdges \cap UnresolvedEdges = {}
