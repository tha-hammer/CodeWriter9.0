The counterexample confirms the diagnosis: the `TransitionWithoutTrace` branch causes TLC to violate `TraceComplete` (step_count=1, trace_log still empty after the violation-path step). The test harness treats any TLC invariant failure as a spec failure. The template's job is to define correct invariants — it must model **only the compliant path** so TLC verifies the invariants hold. The violation semantics are expressed through the invariant definitions themselves, not a deliberately broken `either` branch.
