CONSTANTS
    Rules,
    Edges,
    EdgeCallee,
    EdgeCaller,
    FnOuts,
    RuleMatches,
    RuleRequiredOuts,
    DefaultVal

ASSUME RuleMatches \subseteq Rules \X Edges
